Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026 | Application: Notification Vault

At MS Cyentic Studio, we build applications that respect your data and prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the Notification Vault mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Notification Vault does not upload or share any of your notifications, messages, or device details.

The application operates under a strict offline-first, local-only model:

  • No Network Sync: The application does not communicate with external servers. All logged notification content is saved locally on your device in a secure SQLite database.
  • No Developer Access: Since there is no background synchronization or internet communication, the developers of Notification Vault cannot access, read, or analyze your logs.
  • No User Accounts: No signup, phone registration, or emails are collected.

2. Device Permissions

To perform its core utility function, the application requires specific system permissions. We only request permissions necessary to execute features you actively configure:

  • Notification Listener Access (NotificationListenerService): Required to detect incoming notifications, read their headers/body, and store them locally inside the app. This permission is extremely sensitive, and Android protects it heavily. No data processed through this service is transmitted off the device.
  • Storage/Media Access: Optional, used to allow users to export/import backup files of their notification history.

3. Security of Your Data

Your notification log database is stored inside the app's protected sandbox directory. It cannot be accessed by other standard applications on your device. We recommend utilizing device-level locks (PIN/biometrics) to secure your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children, and we do not knowingly request data or compile information on children, especially since the app has no registration mechanism or online interface.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the app is offline and does not collect data, any changes will be to clarify policies or adapt to new app features. We recommend checking this page periodically for updates.
