Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026 | Application: Tile Match

At MS Cyentic Studio, we build applications that respect your data and prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the Tile Match mobile game application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Tile Match does not collect, record, track, or share any personal information.

The game operates entirely offline:

  • No Network Connection Needed: The app does not require a constant network connection to run, and does not synchronize details with external services.
  • No Ads or Trackers: We do not include any advertisement SDKs, analytics tracking networks, or behavior monitoring tools.
  • Local Progress Saving: Your high scores, current levels, and customization tokens are stored directly on your local device storage using standard Android shared preferences.

2. Device Permissions

Tile Match operates without requesting high-risk system permissions (such as location, camera, contacts, or notification listener logs).

3. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data or transmit tracking profiles, Tile Match is fully safe for children and matches all requirements for family-friendly Play Store listings.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the game is offline and does not collect data, any changes will be to clarify policies or adapt to new app features. We recommend checking this page periodically for updates.
