Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026 | Application: Pocket Zen

At MS Cyentic Studio, we build applications that respect your data and prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the Pocket Zen mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Pocket Zen does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The application operates entirely offline:

  • No User Accounts: You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide any email address or social profile to use the app.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not include any tracking software, third-party software development kits (SDKs), or advertisements that monitor your behavior.
  • Local Data Storage: Any app settings, session history, or goals you configure are stored strictly on your local device storage. This data is not uploaded to any external server.

2. Device Permissions

Pocket Zen may request access to certain system features to provide its core experience. All permissions are stored locally and can be toggled in your Android system settings:

  • Notification Permissions: Used to schedule optional mindfulness reminders (e.g., daily meditation prompts). Reminders are triggered locally by the operating system, not by an external server.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does not communicate with any third-party services. There are no links, tracking pixels, or integrations with external social media networks.

4. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data or run tracking systems, our application is safe for users of all ages, including children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the app is offline and does not collect data, any changes will be to clarify policies or adapt to new app features. We recommend checking this page periodically for updates.
