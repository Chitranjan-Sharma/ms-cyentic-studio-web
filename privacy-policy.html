Company Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026 | MS Cyentic Studio

At MS Cyentic Studio, we are committed to providing simple, private, and reliable applications for Android. We believe that technology should serve you without compromising your personal space, security, or identity. This Privacy Policy details our approach to privacy across all of our applications and services.

App-Specific Privacy Policies

We are currently designing and building offline-first utility applications and casual games.

Individual privacy policies for each application will be published here as soon as they are released on the Google Play Store. Stay tuned!

1. Zero Data Collection & Adherence to Privacy

Our core philosophy is offline-first and local execution. We design our utility apps and casual games to function fully within the sandbox of your device:

  • We do not set up remote user accounts or track your identity.
  • We do not collect, transmit, upload, or sell your name, email, contact list, device location, search history, or any other personal data.
  • Any input data or configurations created within our apps stay stored locally in your device's isolated application storage.

2. Network & Storage Permissions

Our applications request the minimum number of device permissions required to function. For example:

  • Storage/Access permissions: Only requested if an app needs to save local files or configurations directly controlled by you.
  • Notification Access: Request is only made by utility tools (like Notification Vault) that explicitly require it to function locally. We never transmit notifications to any servers.

3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

We do not integrate telemetry, crash loggers, or analytics engines that send identifying profiles to third parties. We strive to maintain clean, tracker-free builds.

4. Children's Privacy

Our applications are completely safe for children. Since we do not collect any personal data, we meet all standards for family-friendly utilities and casual gaming.
